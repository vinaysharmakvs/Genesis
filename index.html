Parent Questions

Before you visit Genesis.

Quick answers for parents who want a structured, serious and caring academic environment.

Which classes does Genesis support?

Genesis supports Classes 8-12 for CBSE and ICSE, along with focused preparation for Boards, JEE, NEET and NDA.

How do parents stay updated?

Parents receive regular academic updates, test feedback and can meet the Academic Head during Saturday Parent Hour.

What makes the Genesis system different?

The system combines phones-at-entry discipline, weekly tests, oral vivas, syllabus completion targets, mindset workshops and parent alignment.
